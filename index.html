What is "SURVIVOR"?

You pick up any newspaper, you will definitely find one or the other news related crime that occurred against women. It is a harsh reality that the women who was once considered as goddess in our country, is not safe on her own land, among her own people.

We at SPARK Foundation have started an initiative titled "SURVIVOR". This aims at providing work opportunities to women in need, women who have been illtreated, dis-respected, molested, or harmed in any way or even in search of a reputed living can apply for these jobs and become independent. Apart from this we conduct many conferences and sessions promoting women empowerment. We also bring to you Women Safety products at the lowest possible prices.

The money you donate to us will be used for creating more work opportunities, conduct more seminars,and we will further transfer some amount to the NGOs working for this cause.
